Our Story

No. 24 Bakery is a family-run bakery based in the Bigg Market area of Newcastle. We pride ourselves of providing an extensive variety of freshly baked goods using the finest ingredients to our local community. With our shop on Cloth Market, as well as our online store, we are able to cater to all patrons, from individual purchase to large group orders.

We also offer a blog where you can keep up to date with the latest news, as well as baking tips and tricks, so you can enjoy our delicious treats in the comfort of your own home.
